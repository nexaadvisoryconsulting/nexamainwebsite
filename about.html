Our Mission

Our Vision

To become Nepal's most trusted financial advisory partner, recognized for excellence, innovation, and unwavering commitment to client success. We envision a business landscape where every enterprise has access to world-class financial expertise.

Our Story

From a vision to Nepal's trusted financial partner

NEXA Advisory & Consulting was founded in 2016 by a team of Chartered Accountants who recognized a gap in the Nepali market – businesses needed more than just number-crunchers; they needed strategic partners who understood both local regulations and global best practices.

What started as a small practice in Kathmandu has grown into a full-service advisory firm serving over 100 clients across diverse industries. Our growth has been powered by word-of-mouth referrals from satisfied clients who appreciate our meticulous attention to detail, proactive advice, and genuine care for their success.

"We don't just prepare financial statements – we help businesses understand their numbers and make better decisions. That's the NEXA difference."

— Rajesh Sharma, Managing Partner

Today, our team includes CAs, CPAs, MBAs, and tax specialists who share a common passion: helping Nepali businesses thrive through financial clarity. We've built a culture of continuous learning, integrity, and client-first thinking that sets us apart.
