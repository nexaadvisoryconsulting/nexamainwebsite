Recruitment FAQs

How do I apply?

Email your CV and cover letter to careers@nexa.com.np with the job title in the subject line.

What's the interview process?

Typically 2-3 rounds: HR screening, technical interview, and final discussion.

Do you offer internships?

Yes, we offer paid internships throughout the year for students and recent graduates.

What qualifications do you look for?

Relevant degree, professional certifications (CA/CPA preferred), and strong analytical skills.
