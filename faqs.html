Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions

What is NEXA Advisory & Consulting?

NEXA Advisory & Consulting is a premier financial services firm based in Kathmandu, Nepal. We provide comprehensive accounting, tax, compliance, and advisory solutions to startups, SMEs, professionals, and established enterprises. Our team includes Chartered Accountants, CPAs, and MBAs with deep expertise in Nepali financial regulations and global best practices.

Where are you located? Do you serve clients outside Kathmandu?

Our headquarters is in Kathmandu, but we serve clients across Nepal through virtual meetings, secure document portals, and occasional onsite visits. We have successfully worked with clients in Pokhara, Biratnagar, Butwal, and even internationally for NRIs and foreign investors.

What makes NEXA different from other accounting firms?

Five key differences set us apart:

  • Strategic Partnership: We don't just crunch numbers; we provide actionable insights to help you grow.
  • Technology-Driven: We use modern tools for efficiency and real-time reporting.
  • Transparent Pricing: No hidden fees, clear proposals, and flexible engagement models.
  • Qualified Team: CAs, CPAs, and MBAs with industry experience.
  • Guaranteed Deadlines: We never miss compliance deadlines – guaranteed.
Is NEXA registered and recognized by professional bodies?

Yes, our firm and team members are affiliated with the Institute of Chartered Accountants of Nepal (ICAN), Institute of Chartered Accountants of India (ICAI), and CPA Australia. We adhere to the highest professional and ethical standards mandated by these bodies.

Services & Offerings

What specific services does NEXA offer?

We offer a comprehensive range of financial services:

  • Accounting & Bookkeeping: Monthly books, financial statements, MIS reports, Tally support
  • Tax Filing & Compliance: VAT, income tax, TDS filing, tax planning, notice representation
  • Company Registration: Private Limited, partnership, PAN/VAT registration, MOA/AOA drafting
  • Financial Advisory: Budgeting, forecasting, investment analysis, business valuation
  • Virtual CFO Services: Strategic planning, cash flow management, fundraising support
  • Payroll Management: Salary processing, TDS, PF/ESI compliance
  • Training & Development: Excel, financial modeling, accounting software training
  • Startup Advisory: Pitch decks, financial modeling, investor connect
Do you offer industry-specific services?

Absolutely. We have tailored solutions for manufacturing, retail & e-commerce, tourism & hospitality, IT & technology, construction & real estate, healthcare, education, transportation, and professional services. Each industry has unique financial requirements, and our team understands the nuances of your sector.

What is Virtual CFO service? Who needs it?

Virtual CFO service provides strategic financial leadership on a part-time, remote basis. It's ideal for growing businesses that need high-level financial expertise but aren't ready for a full-time CFO. Services include financial strategy, cash flow management, fundraising support, board reporting, and investor relations. Many SMEs and funded startups benefit from this service.

Do you help with business registration and licensing?

Yes, we handle end-to-end company registration including Private Limited, partnership firms, PAN/VAT registration, and industry-specific licenses (e.g., tourism, import/export, health). We ensure all paperwork is correctly filed so you can focus on launching your business.

Pricing & Payment

How do you charge for your services?

We offer flexible pricing models based on your needs:

  • Monthly Retainer: Fixed monthly fee for ongoing services (accounting, tax filing, payroll).
  • Project-Based: One-time fee for specific projects (company registration, business valuation, training).
  • Hourly Consulting: For advisory projects with variable scope.
  • Custom Packages: We can create a bundled package combining multiple services.

All pricing is discussed upfront with a detailed proposal – no hidden fees.

Do you require long-term contracts?

No, we don't lock clients into long-term contracts. Our monthly retainer services can be cancelled with 30 days' notice. For project work, payment is tied to project milestones. We believe in earning your business every month through quality service.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept bank transfers, cheques, and digital payments (eSewa, Khalti, ConnectIPS) for your convenience. For international clients, we accept wire transfers in major currencies.

Are there any setup fees or hidden costs?

We believe in complete transparency. Our proposals clearly outline all costs – including any one-time setup fees (if applicable) and monthly charges. There are no hidden costs. We'll also inform you in advance if any government fees (registration, filing) are applicable.

Process & Onboarding

How do I get started with NEXA?

Getting started is simple:

  1. Free Consultation: Schedule a call or meeting to discuss your needs.
  2. Proposal: We'll provide a detailed proposal with scope, timeline, and pricing.
  3. Onboarding: After your approval, we'll send an engagement letter and request initial documents.
  4. Kick-off: We'll set up systems, introduce your dedicated team, and begin work.

Most clients are onboarded within 3-5 business days.

What documents do you need from me to start?

The document list varies by service, but generally includes:

  • Company registration certificate (if registered)
  • PAN/VAT certificates
  • Last 12 months bank statements
  • Previous tax returns (if any)
  • List of directors/partners with KYC documents
  • Any existing accounting data or software access

We provide a comprehensive checklist during onboarding.

How do we communicate and share documents securely?

We use multiple secure channels:

  • Encrypted Portal: For uploading and downloading sensitive documents.
  • WhatsApp/Viber/Telegram: For quick updates and queries.
  • Email: For formal communications.
  • Video Calls: Zoom/Google Meet for regular reviews.
  • Phone: For urgent matters.

All data is encrypted and stored securely in compliance with privacy regulations.

How often will we meet or communicate?

During the first month (onboarding), we may communicate weekly. After that, we schedule monthly review meetings to discuss financials, compliance updates, and strategic matters. However, you can reach out anytime via phone or WhatsApp for urgent queries – we're available 24/7 for our clients.

Compliance & Legal

How do you ensure compliance with Nepali tax laws?

Our team includes experienced tax professionals who stay updated on all changes from the Inland Revenue Department (IRD). We use a compliance calendar to track all deadlines, conduct regular internal reviews, and provide proactive advice to ensure you're always compliant. We also represent you before tax authorities if any notices are received.

What happens if I miss a tax deadline while working with NEXA?

If we are responsible for missing a deadline (e.g., delayed filing), we will cover any late fees or penalties incurred. This is our guarantee. However, we require that you provide necessary documents and information to us in a timely manner as per our agreed schedule.

Is my financial data safe with NEXA?

Absolutely. Data security is our top priority. We implement:

  • Encrypted document portals for all file transfers
  • Two-factor authentication on all systems
  • Strict access controls – only your dedicated team can access your data
  • Confidentiality agreements signed by all team members
  • Regular security audits and backups

We never share your data with third parties without your explicit consent.

Can NEXA represent me before tax authorities if I receive a notice?

Yes, we provide full representation before the Inland Revenue Department (IRD) and other government authorities for all compliance matters handled by us. If you receive any notice, we will review it, prepare the response, and represent you if required. This service is included in our ongoing compliance packages.

What records do I need to maintain for tax purposes?

Under Nepali tax laws, you need to maintain:

  • Books of accounts (cash book, ledger, journal)
  • Purchase and sales invoices/bills
  • Bank statements and deposit slips
  • Expense vouchers and supporting documents
  • Contracts and agreements
  • Fixed asset register
  • Payroll records

If we handle your accounting, we'll maintain all these records for you and provide access anytime.
