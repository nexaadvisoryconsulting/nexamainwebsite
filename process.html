Detailed Process Timeline

Every engagement follows our proven methodology

01

Discovery & Consultation

We begin with an in-depth consultation to understand your business model, financial goals, and specific challenges. This is where we listen and learn.

  • Initial call or meeting (30-60 mins)
  • Business assessment & document review
  • Identify pain points & opportunities
  • Define scope of engagement
Timeline: 2-3 business days
02

Strategic Planning

Based on our discovery, we develop a customized strategic plan with clear objectives, timelines, and deliverables. You'll see exactly what we'll do and when.

  • Custom roadmap development
  • Resource planning & team assignment
  • Technology/tool recommendations
  • Transparent pricing proposal
Timeline: 3-5 business days
03

Implementation

Our experts execute the agreed-upon solutions. Whether it's setting up accounting systems, filing taxes, or providing advisory, we work efficiently with minimal disruption to your operations.

  • System setup & data migration
  • Process documentation
  • Regular progress updates
  • Quality assurance checks
Timeline: Varies by scope (1-4 weeks)
04

Ongoing Support & Optimization

We provide continuous support, regular financial reviews, and proactive advice to ensure your operations remain efficient and compliant as your business grows.

  • Monthly/quarterly reviews
  • 24/7 support availability
  • Regular compliance updates
  • Strategic growth advisory
Timeline: Ongoing partnership

Questions About Our Process

Common queries from clients

How long does onboarding take?

Most clients are fully onboarded within 1-2 weeks. Complex engagements may take up to 4 weeks. We work at your pace.

How often will we communicate?

Weekly updates during implementation, monthly review meetings thereafter. You'll have direct access to your team via calls, WhatsApp, and email.

What documents do you need from me?

We provide a detailed checklist based on your services. Typically: PAN/VAT certificates, bank statements, past returns, and business registrations.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely. We use encrypted portals for document sharing and strictly follow confidentiality agreements.

Can I change services mid-engagement?

Yes, our process is flexible. We can adjust the scope as your needs evolve.

What if I'm not satisfied?

We have a 100% satisfaction guarantee. If something isn't working, we'll make it right.
